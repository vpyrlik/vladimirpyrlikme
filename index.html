Vladimir Pyrlik

Hi! I am Vladimir, a PhD in Economics candidate at CERGE-EI. I expect to be in the market in 2022.

My current research is in the filed of high-dimensional econometrics with application to empirical financial time series analysis. Particularly, in my job market paper, I suggest a novel approach to estimation of skew-t copulas that is appropriate for high and ultra-high dimensions, and potentially improves multivariate models of assets returns distributions.

My other research interests include, yet are not limited to, practical financial forecasting and market volatility analysis.
